General Questions
JobDetect is an AI-powered system that detects fake job postings using 3 methods:
  • Text Detection: Paste the job description — AI will analyze it
  • Image Detection: Upload a screenshot of the job posting
  • URL Verification: Check the job site link
💡 Our ML model is trained on 17,000+ real and fake jobs and delivers 97%+ accuracy!

About Detection
Minimum 50 characters and maximum 5000 characters are required. More text means more accurate results.
💡 For best results, paste the complete job description — include title, requirements, and benefits!
These formats are supported:
  • JPG / JPEG
  • PNG
Maximum size: 5MB
💡 Take a clear, high-resolution screenshot — blurry images prevent OCR from reading text accurately!
The risk score shows the probability that a job posting is fake:
  • 🟢 0-30% — Low Risk: Job is likely real, but still verify independently
  • 🟡 31-70% — Medium Risk: Suspicious indicators present, check carefully
  • 🔴 71-100% — High Risk: Job is highly suspicious, avoid immediately!
⚠️ Low risk does not mean 100% safe — always verify the company independently!
URL verification checks the following:
  • 🔒 SSL Certificate: Whether the website is secure
  • 📅 Domain Age: New domain = suspicious
  • 📋 WHOIS Data: Domain owner information
  • 🚫 Blacklist: Whether it appears in known scam databases
  • ⭐ Trust Score: Overall safety score from 0 to 100
Our Random Forest model achieves 97.47% accuracy.
  • Dataset: 17,880 real and fake job postings (Kaggle)
  • Training: 80% data for training, 20% for testing
  • Features: TF-IDF vectorization (1500 features)
  • 5 models compared: Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, SVM
💡 Random Forest achieved the best F1-Score and was therefore selected!
Currently our model is optimized for English text because the training dataset was in English. Roman Urdu may partially work. Pure Urdu (Arabic script) results will not be accurate.
💡 For best results, use English text or translate the job description before pasting!

Safety Tips
These are the most common red flags:
  • 💰 Asking for money: "Registration fee", "training fee" — NEVER pay!
  • 💸 Unrealistic salary: "$5000/week, no experience needed" — impossible
  • 📱 WhatsApp/personal email: Legitimate companies use official email addresses
  • ⚡ Urgent hiring: "Apply within 24 hours!" — pressure tactic
  • 🏢 Vague company info: No company name, address, or phone number
  • 📝 No interview: Job offer without any interview — highly suspicious!
⚠️ If even one red flag exists — STOP and verify before proceeding!
Follow these steps immediately:
  • 1️⃣ Report on JobDetect — click the "Report This Job" button
  • 2️⃣ Report on the platform — flag it on Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.
  • 3️⃣ File a complaint with FIA Cybercrime — 0800-02345
  • 4️⃣ Warn your friends and family — spread awareness!
💡 If you already made a payment, immediately contact your bank and report the transaction!
Trusted job platforms:
  • ✅ rozee.pk — Pakistan's largest job portal
  • ✅ LinkedIn — Professional network with verified companies
  • ✅ indeed.com — Global platform with user reviews
  • ✅ glassdoor.com — Includes company reviews and ratings
  • ✅ Company website directly — Always the safest option!
⚠️ Be extra careful when applying through WhatsApp groups and Facebook pages!
NEVER share this information — especially during initial stages:
  • 🚫 National ID / Passport number
  • 🚫 Bank account details
  • 🚫 Credit or Debit card information
  • 🚫 Passwords or PINs
  • 🚫 OTP or verification codes
💡 Only your name, email, phone number, and skills are needed in a CV — your ID number is never required at the application stage!

Technical Questions
Our system uses a Random Forest Classifier which:
  • Extracts text features using TF-IDF vectorization
  • Was selected from 5 models based on the best F1-Score
  • Handles imbalanced data using class_weight='balanced'
  • Trained on Kaggle dataset: 17,880 job postings
If the system is slow, try the following:
  • 🔄 Refresh the page
  • 🌐 Check your internet connection
  • 💻 Make sure the backend server is running — run py app.py in the terminal
  • 🧹 Clear your browser cache
💡 URL verification may be slower as it performs live internet checks in real time!
Common solutions for OCR errors:
  • 📸 Use a clear image: Blurry images cause OCR to fail
  • 📏 Good resolution: Minimum 800px width is recommended
  • 💡 Good lighting: Avoid dark or low-contrast screenshots
  • 📄 Text must be visible: Ensure good contrast between text and background
💡 If image detection is not working, copy the text and use Text Detection instead!
